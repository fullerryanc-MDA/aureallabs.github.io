Our Mission

Aureal Labs builds tools that empower detectorists to explore responsibly — protecting heritage, respecting property rights, and preserving the stories beneath our feet.

We believe technology should enhance discovery, not exploit it. That's why Aureal prioritizes privacy, ethical reporting, and transparency at every step.
